What this means for your payroll.

From 6 April 2027, company cars, car fuel, vans, van fuel and employer-provided medical benefits must be reported through payroll in real time via the Full Payment Submission.

Most other benefits in kind are expected to follow from April 2028.

Employment-related loans and living accommodation stay outside the mandatory regime, so the P11D process continues for those.
